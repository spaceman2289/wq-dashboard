Lake Worth Lagoon Water Quality Dashboard

This dashboard displays surface freshwater input and salinity data for the Lake Worth Lagoon.

Flow data for all sites and salinity data for sites LWL-19 and LWL-20A are provided by the South Florida Water Management District through the DBHYDRO environmental database.

Salinity data for John's Island and Munyon Island are provided by Palm Beach County's Department of Environmental Resources Management.
